Shipping Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Order Processing

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays. If we are experiencing a high volume of orders, shipments may be delayed by a few days.

2. Shipping Rates & Delivery Estimates

We offer free standard shipping on all prepaid orders across India.

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 Business days
  • Expedited Shipping: Available on request (Extra charges apply)

Delivery delays can occasionally occur depending on the courier partner's load and your location (Tier 2/3 cities might take slightly longer).

3. Shipment Confirmation & Order Tracking

You will receive a Shipment Confirmation email or WhatsApp message once your order has shipped containing your tracking number(s). The tracking number will be active within 24 hours.

4. Damages

ArtVista is liable for any products damaged or lost during shipping. If you received your order damaged, please contact us immediately (within 24 hours of delivery) so we can file a claim and send you a replacement.

Please save all packaging materials and damaged goods before filing a claim.
