Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you visit ArtVista, we collect certain information about your device, your interaction with the Site, and information necessary to process your purchases. We may also collect additional information if you contact us for customer support.

  • Order Information: Name, billing address, shipping address, payment information (processed securely via Razorpay), email address, and phone number.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the Order Information that we collect generally to fulfill any orders placed through the Site (including processing your payment information, arranging for shipping, and providing you with invoices and/or order confirmations).

We also use this information to communicate with you regarding your order status via Email or WhatsApp.

3. Third-Party Services

We share your Personal Information with third parties to help us use your Personal Information, as described above.

  • Payments: We use Razorpay to process payments securely. We do not store your credit card details on our servers.
  • Shipping: We share your address and phone number with our courier partners (e.g., Delhivery, Xpressbees) solely for delivery purposes.

4. Data Retention

When you place an order through the Site, we will maintain your Order Information for our records unless and until you ask us to delete this information.
